The visionary behind Ochieng Enterprises

Eric Ochieng

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

With vast experience in the decoration industry, Eric Ochieng Opiyo founded Ochieng Enterprises with a vision to transform spaces into works of art. His passion for design and attention to detail has made the company a leader in luxury decorations.

Eric believes that every space tells a story, and his mission is to help clients create environments that reflect their personality and style while maintaining the highest standards of quality and elegance.

Under his leadership, Ochieng Enterprises has completed hundreds of successful projects for both residential and commercial clients, earning a reputation for excellence and innovation in the decoration industry.
